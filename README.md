
# Vincent's Archive

A beautifully crafted, immersive digital archive celebrating the life, works, and letters of the legendary post-impressionist painter, Vincent van Gogh.

> "I dream of painting and then I paint my dream."

## 🎨 Overview

Vincent's Archive is an interactive React application designed to provide a cohesive and emotive experience when exploring Van Gogh's legacy. Experience fine art alongside atmospheric music and curated insights into his turbulent yet profoundly beautiful life.

## 🚀 Live Demo

[Visit the Live Archive →](https://vincent-cyan.vercel.app/)

## ✨ Key Features

-   **Immersive Hero Interface:** A dynamic, split-screen home experience featuring high-quality imagery of Van Gogh's iconic landscapes.
-   **Artistic Masterpieces:** A curated, responsive gallery of Van Gogh's most significant works with contextual details.
-   **Atmospheric Audio:** Built-in ambient & thematic musical experience ("Vincent") with a gentle fade-in feature to set the mood, and a customizable toggle.
-   **Structured History:** Dedicated sections for Biography, Timeline, and Letters.
-   **Interactive Correspondence:** A "Write to Vincent" section for deeper reflection.
